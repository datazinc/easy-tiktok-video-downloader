Easy TikTok Profile Download

To downoad videos, navigate to the profile page, copy all links and paste them into your downloader.

hint 1: Google how to do batch download with XDM, IDM, uGet etc.

hint 2: To download a single video click on its link and then use the right-click save trick.

hint 3: To get all profile videos keep scrolling down until all videos are loaded.

hint 4: (you might need to)Rename downloaded video to end with .mp4 extension.

FAQ:

Could you give links to videos without watermarks?

No. This tool is built to only use information provided to your browser from TikTok. Non-watermark links can only be optained(AFAIK) using a server-side solution. If there is an easy way to retrieve it without the use of a third party service, send me a feature request with instructions.
